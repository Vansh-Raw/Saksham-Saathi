Getting Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with a social issue, it's important to seek help. At Saksham Saathi, we provide resources and support to help you navigate these challenges. Here are some ways to get help:

Contact a trusted friend or family member

Reach out to someone you trust and feel comfortable talking to. This could be a friend, family member, or mentor. Let them know what you're going through and ask for their support.

Reach out to a mental health professional

Consider seeking help from a mental health professional, such as a therapist, counselor, or psychologist. They can provide you with a safe and confidential space to talk about your experiences and help you develop coping strategies.

Call a helpline or support hotline

There are many helplines and support hotlines available that can provide immediate help and support. These services are often available 24/7 and can connect you with a trained professional who can listen to your concerns and provide guidance.

  • National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS) Helpline (India)
  • iCall Psychological Helpline (India)
  • Aasra Suicide Prevention Helpline (India)
  • Sneha Suicide Prevention Helpline (India)

Access online resources andsupport groups

There are many online resources and support groups available that can provide you with information, advice, and a sense of community. These resources can help you learn more about your issue and connect with others who are going through similar experiences.

  • Mental Health India
  • The Health Site Mental Health
  • Indian Women Blog Mental Health
  • The Live Inspired

Emergency Services

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, please call your local emergency services number.
